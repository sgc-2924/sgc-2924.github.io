Welcome to UVEMS

The Unique Vision English Medium School was founded in 2007 by the late Mr. Gajanan P. Chinchwade. It is affiliated with the state board of education, with classes ranging from nursery to standard 10th. It is a private school and is managed by Pawana Education Social Trust.
We focus on all-round development, and we have consistently managed to inculcate good values in our students to align with our motto, "Conquer the World With Unique Vision."
